Every great opinion starts with a good view.

Opinions on Tap is a pub life brand built around one idea — the pint glass as a lens on the world.

The Idea

There's something about a pub that makes people honest.

Not the alcohol — the atmosphere. The fact that everyone's sitting down, no one's in a hurry, and the conversation has room to go somewhere.

That's what Opinions on Tap is about. The pint glass is our way of framing it. When you're holding one, you're not rushing. You're looking around. You've got a point of view.

We took that idea literally. Every design puts a real place or moment inside a pint glass. A coastline. A course. A view worth stopping for. Because the best views and the best opinions tend to show up at the same time.

The Series

The Pint of View series starts in Australia.

Each shirt captures something worth raising a glass to — real locations, great courses, iconic coastlines, places and moments that mean something to the people who've been there.

The series grows. The views change. The pint stays cold.
